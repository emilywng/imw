Project 1: Storybook Process

The Story I Chose:

For this project, I was inspired by my cat to tell a story about the life
of a simple house cat. For many cats, their most favourite activity is
to eat, so I figured I would create a visual narrative of that.
I was inspired by the example of Tess Smith Roberts' side by side
frame style. I wanted to have the colourful shapes in the background
to be clickable for the user to reveal the next frame if they click the
correct shape. Unfortunately I couldn't get this to work, so I improvised
and incorporated the interactive function in the captions of the frames.

I encountered a couple obstacles and had to change my drawings a bit. I was having trouble figuring
out how to create curved lines. I managed to find an example online, but the mouth didn't turn out as
curved as I wanted to.
